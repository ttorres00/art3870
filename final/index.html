The Bistro

The Bistro, opened by its current owners, Vic and Cynthia Kralj, in January 1994. The space went through a major face change in June of the same year. Formerly known as the Vintage Cellar Wine Shop, The Bistro soon established itself as a major Hop Spot and Music Venue in the East Bay. We proudly serve the community of Hayward with locally roasted coffee, fresh soups & sandwiches, fine wine & handcrafted ales and lagers.
